## Story

**As a** visitor browsing NamaSer on mobile,
**I want** a smoother testimonials experience, visible payment trust signals, and a footer that matches the site's warm tone,
**so that** I feel confident through every section and the experience feels polished end-to-end.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Carousel autoplay: options presented to user (disable on mobile OR increase to 8s)
2. Duplicate "Vozes" section evaluated — options presented: consolidate into 1 with tabs/filters OR keep 2 with clear differentiation
3. Accordion allows multiple items open simultaneously
4. Payment badges implemented near pricing section — visual options approved by user and @ux-design-expert
5. Footer redesigned with warm colors aligned to site tone — options approved by user and @ux-design-expert
6. CTA buttons have `:active` state (`scale(0.98)`) for touch feedback
7. All visual changes approved by @ux-design-expert and user BEFORE implementation
8. Any new text approved by squad Light Copy

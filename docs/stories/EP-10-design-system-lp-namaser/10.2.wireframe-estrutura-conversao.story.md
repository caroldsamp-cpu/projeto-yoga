## Story

**As a** product owner (Gaby),
**I want** a detailed wireframe of the NamaSer sales landing page with a conversion-optimized structure that respects the brand's welcoming tone,
**so that** the page guides visitors naturally from awareness to purchase without aggressive tactics.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Wireframe defines all LP sections in order, with purpose of each section documented
2. Each section includes: content type, approximate copy direction, CTA placement
3. Conversion structure follows proven patterns adapted for NamaSer tone (no urgency, no guilt, invitation-based)
4. Hero section has clear value proposition aligned with messaging house
5. Social proof section uses real testimonials (text + video placeholder)
6. FAQ section addresses real objections from lead survey (55 leads)
7. CTA language follows brand guide (acolhedor, sem pressao)
8. Mobile-first layout considerations documented
9. Wireframe includes annotations explaining WHY each section is in that position (conversion rationale)
10. Document saved at `docs/stories/EP-10-design-system-lp-namaser/wireframe-lp-namaser.md`

### Padroes de conversao a adaptar (NAO copiar literalmente)
- Hero com proposta de valor clara
- Secao de "para quem e" (identificacao)
- Beneficios tangives (o que muda no dia a dia)

- Detalhes do produto (trilhas, modulos)
- Pricing transparente
- Garantia
- FAQ enderecanfo objecoes reais
- CTA final

### O que NAO fazer (guardrails)
- Contagem regressiva / urgencia artificial
- "Ultimas vagas" / escassez falsa
- Linguagem de culpa
- Promessas de transformacao instantanea
- Tom de coach agressivo

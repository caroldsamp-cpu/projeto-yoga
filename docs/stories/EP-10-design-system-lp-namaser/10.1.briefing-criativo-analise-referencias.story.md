## Story

**As a** product owner (Gaby),
**I want** a unified creative briefing that compiles all brand positioning, ICP data, tone of voice, and visual reference analysis into one actionable document,
**so that** all subsequent design and LP work is anchored in validated brand strategy and clear visual direction.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Briefing compiles positioning from `docs/marca/posicionamento-namaser.md`, `brand-guide-namaser.md`, and `messaging-house-namaser.md`
2. ICP data from `docs/estrategia/icp-namaser.md` and `docs/research/lead-survey-55-namaser-2026-02-21.md` is summarized with key pain points, desires, and language patterns
3. Gaby's tone of voice is extracted from `docs/design-system/REVISAO-TOM-GABY.md` and `docs/analise-gaby/10-Gaby-Clone-Manual-Completo.md` with clear do/don't guidelines
4. Visual analysis of 3 reference sites (gabydrummondyoga.com.br, maas-hub.vercel.app, framework-arquiteto-ia.vercel.app) documenting: color palette, typography, spacing, hierarchy, CTAs, imagery — with what to adopt and what to skip
5. Visual direction section defines: light/luminous palette, elegant typography, generous spacing — adapted for yoga/wellness context
6. Document includes "Guardrails Visuais" section listing what is FORBIDDEN (dark theme, aggressive urgency, guilt language, performance yoga imagery)
7. All content traces back to existing project documents (no invented information)
8. Document saved at `docs/stories/EP-10-design-system-lp-namaser/briefing-criativo-namaser.md`
